<p>This game is an early beta sandbox survival adventure set in a two dimansional procedurally generated voxel world. Players can explore diverse landscapes, including caves and biomes, gather resources, craft tools, armor, and food, and build structures to survive. The world features a day/night cycle, dynamic lighting, fluids, and interactive mobs.</p>

<p><strong>Features include:</strong></p>
<ul>
  <li>Procedural world generation with ores, caves, hills, and biomes.</li>
  <li>Crafting system with recipes for tools, armor, and consumables.</li>
  <li>Animals and hostile mobs with unique behaviors and drops.</li>
  <li>Dynamic lighting, fluids, and smooth terrain rendering.</li>
  <li>Player actions including building, mining, crafting, combat, and exploration.</li>
  <li>Inventory and survival systems, including food, health, and equipment.</li>
</ul>
